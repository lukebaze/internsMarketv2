## Introduction (5 min)

**"Thank you for joining today. We're testing [product], not you — there are no right or wrong answers. Please think out loud as you work through tasks: tell us what you're looking at, what you're thinking, and what you expect to happen."**

**Warm-up questions:**
1. Can you tell me a bit about your role and how you typically [relevant activity]?
2. How often do you use tools like [product category]?
3. What's your biggest frustration with how you currently [relevant task]?
