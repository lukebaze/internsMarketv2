## Content Calendar Structure

### Monthly Themes
- **Month focus:** One pillar or campaign theme per month
- **Anchor piece:** 1 high-investment piece (original research, long guide, video)
- **Supporting pieces:** 4–8 pieces that surround and link to the anchor

### Weekly Rhythm
- **Monday:** Plan and brief the week's content
- **Tuesday–Thursday:** Write and review
- **Friday:** Schedule and distribute
- **Ongoing:** Monitor performance, capture ideas
