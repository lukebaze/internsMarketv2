## Audience

**Primary reader:**
_Job title, experience level, biggest pain point._
_e.g. "Solo developer, 2–5 years experience, frustrated by DevOps complexity"_

**What they already know:**
_Assumed knowledge — what you don't need to explain._

**What they fear:**
_The outcome they're trying to avoid._
